OUR STORY

नमन — A sanctuary of
timeless Indian elegance

Nestled in the heart of Rajasthan, NAMAN is more than a hotel — it is a living tribute to India’s rich heritage. Every detail, from the hand-carved sandstone walls to the fragrant marigold garlands, reflects centuries of tradition while embracing contemporary luxury.

Whether you seek solitude by the infinity pool overlooking the Aravalli hills or wish to experience a private yoga session at sunrise, NAMAN invites you to slow down and reconnect with yourself.
